What is the Bronco Startup Challenge?

About the Competition

3 Month Business Competition

The Bronco Startup Challenge is a business competition designed to encourage students from different backgrounds, specialties, and expertise to create a unified vision. From ideation to business plan and beyond, the competition is designed to grow the horizons of innovative students. It is about startups, young and innovative companies who aim to disrupt and change the status quos.

$10,000 in cash for your startup idea.

1ST PLACE - $4,000 cash
2ND PLACE - $3,000 cash
3RD PLACE - $2,000 cash
BONUS: CROWD FAVORITE - $1,000 cash

Schedule of the Competition

The structure of the Bronco Startup Challenge is simple. We begin with an kickoff party to start the 3 month long sprint for business ideas, throw several workshops, mixers, and events midway through the process, and wrap up with an awards ceremony.

The Judges and Judging Process

The judges are well-known entrepreneurs and come from all kinds of backgrounds! We will be following a rubric which will judge your business idea on various categories such as innovativeness, scalability, and more.
